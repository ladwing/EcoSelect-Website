How It Works

1

Browse as Usual

Visit your favorite online store. EcoSelect runs quietly in the background while you shop.

2

Auto-Detection

Open the extension. It automatically reads the product title of the item you are currently viewing.

3

Discover Alternatives

Instantly receive a list of highly-rated sustainable options complete with prices and direct links.

Powered by Advanced AI

🧠

Contextual Understanding

EcoSelect uses Google Gemini AI to understand exactly what you are shopping for, going beyond simple keyword matching to find true eco-friendly equivalents.

📍

Smart Localization

Stop looking at products that cannot be shipped to you. EcoSelect specifically targets e-commerce platforms available in your region (whether you are shopping on Noon in the UAE or Amazon in Europe).

⚡

Semantic Caching

Our custom vector database ensures that popular searches are returned in milliseconds without redundant processing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is EcoSelect really free?

Yes. The extension is completely free to download and use.

Can I use my own API keys?

Absolutely. Power users can input their own Gemini and Serper API keys in the extension's settings panel. These keys are stored securely on your local device.

Does this slow down my browser?

Not at all. EcoSelect is designed to be incredibly lightweight and only activates when you open the popup on a supported e-commerce page.
